About

Real baking. Real ingredients. No shortcuts.

Sir SourDough started with learning the craft, chasing better fermentation, stronger crust, and a loaf worth sharing. What matters here is simple: honest food, small batches, and making it right instead of making it fast.

  • Slow fermentation: Time does the heavy lifting.
  • Small-batch workflow: Better control, better quality.
  • Local pickup: Fresh bread for local customers, not warehouse shelves.
