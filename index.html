Sir Sourdough is a small-batch bakery built on patience, simplicity, and doing things the right way. Every loaf is naturally leavened, fermented slowly, and made with ingredients you can actually recognize. This started as a craft, learning the process, dialing it in, and chasing that perfect crumb. Now it’s about sharing it. Fresh bread, honest food, and something real in a world full of shortcuts.
