About the Bakehouse

A bakery built on craft, comfort, and character.

Sir SourDough Baking Co. brings together artisan technique and a rich, rustic tavern feel. The focus is simple: beautiful bread, handmade bakes, and a product that feels personal from the first look to the final bite.

Every bake is treated with care, from the shaping of the dough to the final crust and crumb. The result is a table-ready loaf with old-world presence and handmade soul.
